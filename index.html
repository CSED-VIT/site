About Us

S.H.E.I.L.D is an organistaion originating from VIT, Vellore. It is a platform for budding entrepreneurs to scale up their startup from ideation stage. We help the young generation of entrepreneurs understand the ins and outs of the startup world. In addition to this we hold various events and workshops in schools and colleges to help students ideate in early stage. An important part of our work is to connect entrepreneurs to investors and interns through our growing network, providing them the required opportunities to grow.
