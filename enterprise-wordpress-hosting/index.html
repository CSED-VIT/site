What are the details of the Kinsta Enterprise hosting?

Google Cloud Platform

We chose the Google Cloud Platform for its automatic scalability, state of the art security, multi-regional deployment, and high-performance. Many 3rd party hosts are constrained to 2-6 CPUs per VPS. Utilizing the Google Compute Engine, we actually let the site’s container scale to as many CPUs as needed when there’s a surge of traffic. This ensures your WordPress site stays online 24×7.

Google’s private Fiber network is one of the biggest in the world, allowing our machines to process more data in less time. We utilize Google's premium tier network, unlike other hosts who might opt for the standard tier network to cut costs. All data is stored redundantly, with automatic checksums to ensure data integrity.

HTTP/2 content delivery network

Content delivery networks have been shown to decrease load times by over 50%. They accomplish this by minimizing network latency, geographical distance your data has to travel, and utilizing technolgies such as GZIP compression. We’ve partnered up with KeyCDN, a real-time HTTP/2 powered content delivery network, with 28 POPs around the globe to speed up delivery of your assets (images, JS, CSS). Free bandwidth is included wtih every plan.
